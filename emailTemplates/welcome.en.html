Welcome to Scala1!

Thank you for trying out the Scala1 app.

As you may know, we have released Scala1 as Open Source projects, and we would love to get your feedback. Even better, if you'd like to play with the code, and contribute to it, it's as easy as 1-2-3:

  1. Fork the application repository here.
  2. Hack away at the source code.
  3. Send us a pull request, and we'll include your changes if they're safe and fun for everyone :)

You can also take a look at the iOS and Android repositories.

Feel free to send us any feedback you have.

Have fun, and enjoy the conference!
